Key Message

AMH strongly reflects ovarian response and embryo yield but does not independently predict live birth after single euploid frozen embryo transfer. Day-5 transfer was the only independent positive predictor of live birth.

Interpretation

AMH was strongly associated with oocyte yield and Day-5 embryo number, but the total indirect effect of AMH on live birth was not significant. Day-5 transfer was independently associated with higher live birth odds compared with Day 6.
