Model Interpretation

In the multivariable model, Day 5 transfer was independently associated with higher odds of live birth compared with Day 6 transfer (OR 1.70, 95% CI 1.28–2.27, p < 0.001).

AMH showed a borderline negative association with live birth (OR 0.954, 95% CI 0.91–1.00, p = 0.065), but did not reach statistical significance. Female age, BMI, endometrial thickness, embryo quality, and diagnostic indication were not independently associated with live birth.
